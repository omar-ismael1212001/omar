Artificial General Intelligence


The creation of a machine with human-level intelligence which will be applied to any task is that the chalice for several AI researchers, however the hunt for AGI has been fraught with issue. The hunt for a "universal rule for learning and acting in any setting," (Russel and Norvig 27) is not new, however time hasn't relieved the problem of basically making a machine with a full set of psychological feature skills. AGI has long been the muse of dystopian fantasy, during which super-intelligent robots overrun humanity, however consultants agree it isn't one thing we'd like to stress concerning anytime presently.
